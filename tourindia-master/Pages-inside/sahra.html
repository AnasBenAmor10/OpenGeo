Description

Tozeur, on the edge of the Atlas Mountains, features a vast oasis with 350,000 palm trees. The 15th-century medina, built with light-colored bricks, is best explored on foot or by bicycle. Heading south, cross the reflective expanse of Chott El-Jerid to Douz, a desert gateway with a weekly market and camel rides. Further south lies the Grand Erg Oriental, offering 40,000 square kilometers of ever-shifting dunes. The guide navigates to a camp in the dunes for a night under the stars before hitting the road again.
